About Sharkpen

Precision. Speed. Partnership.

Sharkpen Consulting was founded on a simple conviction: technology should amplify human judgment, not replace it. In a market saturated with generic IT vendors, we operate differently — as a focused, senior-led technical partner that goes deep on your specific environment and challenges.

"The precision of a pen. The momentum of a shark. We move fast, write clean solutions, and don't stop until the problem is solved."

We work with organizations navigating the transition to AI-augmented operations, combining sharp technical execution with a human-first advisory philosophy. Every engagement is led by senior consultants — never delegated to junior staff.

With over 15 years of enterprise IT experience and a specialized AI/ML practice, we bring the depth of a large firm with the responsiveness of a focused boutique.
